Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18

Gym Companion is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that helps users follow safe, structured gym sessions. This page explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data collection

The app does not collect any personal data by default. The following local data may be stored on your device:

  • Session progress and workout state (saved to localStorage).
  • Optional weights/notes you enter while using the app (saved locally).

Analytics & third-party services

By default, no analytics or third-party tracking is embedded. If analytics are added in the future (e.g., Google Analytics), they will be disclosed here and will only be used to improve the app.

Service workers and caching

The app uses a service worker to cache assets for offline use. Cached assets are the app shell files (HTML, CSS, JS, icons) and do not contain personal information.

Hosting & external links

The app is hosted at https://ambijat.github.io/gym-companion/. This privacy policy covers data within the PWA; external links may have different policies.

Publishing to Google Play

If the PWA is packaged and published to Google Play, Play Console metadata and Play's own privacy and data controls apply. The app will continue to operate primarily as a PWA and will not collect personal data unless explicitly added.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact: YOUR_DEVELOPER_EMAIL@example.com

To request deletion of any locally stored data, uninstall the app from your device and clear site data in the browser settings.
